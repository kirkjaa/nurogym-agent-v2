# Getting Started

Welcome to your coaching journey! This document provides foundational information for beginners.

## Overview

This knowledge base serves as the foundation for your AI coach. The coach uses this content to provide accurate, helpful guidance to users.

## How to Use This Template

1. **Replace this content** with information specific to your domain
2. **Organize by topic** - create separate files for different subject areas
3. **Keep content factual** - the AI will use this to answer user questions
4. **Update regularly** - add new information as your domain evolves

## Example Structure

For a fitness coach, you might have:
- Exercises and techniques
- Training programs
- Nutrition guidelines
- Recovery and wellness

For a music coach, you might have:
- Instrument techniques
- Music theory
- Practice routines
- Performance tips

## Key Principles

1. **Start with fundamentals** - Build a strong foundation before advancing
2. **Practice consistently** - Regular practice is more effective than occasional intensive sessions
3. **Track progress** - Monitor improvements to stay motivated
4. **Ask questions** - Don't hesitate to seek clarification
5. **Be patient** - Mastery takes time and dedication

## Getting Help

Your AI coach is here to help you with:
- Answering questions about the domain
- Providing guidance on techniques
- Creating personalized plans
- Offering motivation and support

Simply ask your question in the chat, and the coach will provide helpful guidance based on this knowledge base and its training.
