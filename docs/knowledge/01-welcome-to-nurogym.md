# Welcome to NuroGym

NuroGym is a fitness and wellness app made for people who are neurodivergent. This includes people with:
- **Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)**
- **Down syndrome**
- **Cerebral Palsy**

NuroGym is also for **caregivers, parents, and support workers** who help neurodivergent individuals stay active and healthy.

---

## What is NuroCoach?

NuroCoach is your personal AI fitness companion inside NuroGym. NuroCoach can:

- Suggest fun and safe exercises that match your abilities
- Give step-by-step instructions in simple language
- Help you feel motivated and supported
- Answer questions in **English and Thai (ภาษาไทย)**
- Remind you that rest, fun, and going at your own pace are all part of the journey

---

## Why Fitness Matters for Neurodivergent Individuals

Research shows that people with disabilities are **three times more likely** to develop chronic health problems if they are not physically active. Regular movement can help with:

- **Mood** — Exercise releases feel-good chemicals in the brain
- **Sleep** — Physical activity helps you sleep better at night
- **Focus and attention** — Movement helps the brain stay alert and calm
- **Motor skills** — Practice helps coordination and balance improve over time
- **Social connection** — Group activities and programs help build friendships
- **Energy levels** — Regular exercise helps reduce fatigue over time

---

## NuroGym's Promise

NuroGym is built on three promises:

1. **Safety first** — Every exercise suggestion is designed to be safe. If something feels wrong, stop and rest.
2. **Plain language always** — We explain every fitness term in words that are easy to understand.
3. **Your pace, your rules** — There is no "normal" speed. Every step forward — big or small — is a win.

## How to Use NuroCoach

Simply type a message in the chat. Here are some examples of what you can ask:

- "What exercise can I do today?"
- "I feel tired. What should I do?"
- "What is a good stretch for my back?"
- "I have cerebral palsy. Can you suggest a workout?"
- "My son has Down syndrome. How do we start exercising?"
- "ออกกำลังกายอะไรดีสำหรับวันนี้?" (Thai: What exercise is good for today?)

---

## Who Can Use NuroGym?

NuroGym is designed for individuals aged **24–50** who are neurodivergent, but it is welcoming to anyone. Caregivers and support workers are especially encouraged to use it alongside the individuals they support.

---

## A Note on Medical Advice

NuroCoach provides **general wellness and fitness guidance**. It is not a replacement for medical advice.

Before starting any new exercise program, please check with:
- Your **doctor**
- Your **physiotherapist**
- Your **occupational therapist**

This is especially important if you have a heart condition, epilepsy, bone or joint problems, or any other medical condition that affects exercise.
